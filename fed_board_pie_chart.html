Political Affiliation & Monetary Policy Stance (2025)

Legend & Definitions

Hawk:
Fed officials who prefer higher interest rates to combat inflation, prioritizing price stability over employment growth.
Dove:
Fed officials who prefer lower interest rates to stimulate economic growth and employment, more tolerant of inflation.
